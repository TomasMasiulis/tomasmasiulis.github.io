Brain Speed is an educational game that helps to improve your ability to think fast. Train your brain and keep it fit!

About

Speed of thinking is very important in professional life, in sport and in other areas. Your brain’s innate neuroplasticity allows you to develop this ability. Scientists say that the brain can be compared to a muscle, the more you train it, the stronger it becomes. Brain Speed is the perfect tool to train your brain and keep it fit. At the same time the game is fun and a good way to relax during your lunch break or after a busy day.


The purpose of the game is to find sequences of numbers as fast as you can! On each level the sequences, shapes, colors and arrangement of the numbers are different. Each level of the game is more difficult than the previous one. This will keep you challenged! There are single player and two player modes in the game.

Single player

Single player is designed for individual brain speed training. The main indicator of how good you perform and progress in this mode is your Brain Speed Index (BSI). It is calculated according to the best completion times of each level. If you manage to pass all levels of the game you can still keep on training. This means that if you beat your best time of the level you have already passed, your general BSI will still increase. It is very interesting to compare your performance with other Brain Speed players around the world.

Two player

In a two player (duel) mode, you can play online with other players from all around the world. In this mode you have to find the sequences of numbers faster than your opponents. Both players start the same level and play until one of the players finds all the numbers. You can either play with the particular person or the platform can randomly choose the opponent for you. Success in this mode depends only on the number of victories you manage to achieve.
