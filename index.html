About

Brain Speed is an educational game that helps to improve your ability to think fast. The rules are simple, but it is not so easy to the reach the highest levels. Speed of thinking is very important in your professional life, sports and other areas. Your brainâ€™s innate neuroplasticity allows you to develop this ability. Scientists say that brain can be compared with the muscle, the more you train them, the stronger they become. Brain Speed is a perfect tool to train your brain and keep them fit. At the same time the game can be fun and a good way to relax during your lunch break or after a busy day.


The purpose of the game is to find the sequences of numbers as fast as you can! On each level sequences, shapes, colors and arrangement of numbers are different. Each level of the game is more difficult than the last one. This will keep you challenged! There are single player and two player modes in the game.

Single player

Single player is designed for individual brain speed training. The main indicator of how good you perform and progress in this mode is your Brain Speed Index (BSI). It is calculated according to the best completion times of each level. If you manage to pass all levels of the game you can still keep on training. This means that if you beat your best time of the level you have already passed, your general BSI will still increase. It is very interesting to compare your performance with other Brain Speed players around the world. Just click the icon of the leaderboard and you will see your global ranking according to your BSI.

Two player

In a two player (duel) mode, you can play online with other players from all around the world. In this mode you have to find the sequences of numbers faster than your opponents. Both players start the same level and play until one of the players finds all the numbers. You can either play with the particular person or the platform can randomly choose the opponent for you. Success in this mode depends only on the number of victories you manage to achieve. If you want to compare your performance, just click the icon of the leaderboard and you will see your global ranking according to your victories in this mode.
